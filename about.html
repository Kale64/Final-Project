How I Got Here

I started off at Westgate Community School from Kindergarten to 7th grade, and then changed to Jefferson Academy my 8th grade year. Jefferson Academy is much much better than Westgate for so many reasons. My mental health is a lot better and I am continuing to go to high school at JA.

Who I Am

My name is Kayla Hughes. I am a pretty pink princess. I find many things to be fantastic, and running is one of the them. It is my main hobby, alongside reading, climbing, and drawing. I hope to be friends with you, and I hope my hope is reciprocated!

My Mission

I would love for everyone to start running. I love running, and it is so good for you, it brings me much joy and pain, and very well could be my life's purpose. I really think everyone should try running, and I truly hope they will love it as much as I do.
